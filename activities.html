For thrill-seekers, Heber's close proximity to Deer Valley and Sundance resorts means access to some of the best skiing and snowboarding around. These top-tier resorts offer a range of slopes for all skill levels, paired with breathtaking mountain vistas. Whether you're carving down the fresh powder in winter or taking a scenic hike during the warmer months, the outdoor adventures are endless.

If you're more into relaxation, a visit to the Homestead Crater is a must. This natural hot spring, hidden within a beehive-shaped limestone dome, provides a unique and soothing experience. You can soak in the warm mineral waters, go for a swim, or even try scuba diving in this surreal setting. It's a perfect spot to unwind and enjoy nature's geothermal wonders.

For a taste of local culture and history, catch a performance at the Heber Valley Artisan Cheese.
