At Back 40 Ranch House Grill, you can savor a hearty meal made with fresh, local ingredients from Heber and its surrounding valleys. Their menu features a variety of dishes, from juicy burgers and tender steaks to comforting chicken dishes and flavorful salads. The restaurant's stunning views, especially during sunset, make it a perfect spot to enjoy a meal with friends and family

For a taste of local comfort food, Chick's Cafe is a must-visit. This cozy eatery offers a range of American favorites, including their famous chicken fried steak, fluffy scrambled eggs, and freshly baked scones. The friendly service and reasonable prices add to the welcoming atmosphere, making it a beloved spot for both locals and visitors.

If you're in the mood for a nostalgic treat, head to Dairy Keen - Home of the Train. This charming spot, established in 1946, serves up classic American dishes like hamburgers, salads, and sandwiches. The highlight, however, is their delicious ice cream, with unique flavors that you won't find anywhere else. Enjoy your meal while taking in the historic ambiance of this beloved Heber institution
